About

Pycon Finland is a non-profit conference organized by a non-profit organization called Python Finland association. The conference is meant for people interested in Python software development and related technologies. It's a part of global PyCon conference series

Pycon Finland 2016 is the 7th PyCon Finland and will be held on October 31st 2016.

The venue for the conference will be HTC Helsinki in Ruoholahti. The same as last year.
